## Quick Start

1. **Download the App**
   - [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store) (Android)
   - [Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com) (iOS)

2. **Create Your Account**
   - Sign up with email or social login
   - Complete your profile
   - Start connecting!

3. **Explore Features**
   - Follow users and communities
   - Post your first thread
   - Send encrypted messages
   - Browse the marketplace
