## Privacy Controls

### Who Can See Your Profile

Control your profile visibility:

- **Public** - Anyone can view
- **Followers** - Only approved followers
- **Private** - Invitation only

### Content Filtering

Choose what appears on your profile:

- Show/hide media posts
- Hide sensitive content
- Filter replies
- Block keywords

### Activity Status

Control online status:

- Show "Active Now" badge
- Display "Last Seen" time
- Hide activity completely

## Profile Analytics (For Creators)

Track your profile performance:

### Overview Stats
- Total followers
- Profile views (last 30 days)
- Engagement rate
- VCoin earnings

### Growth Metrics
- New followers per day
- Follower demographics
- Top performing posts
- Best posting times

### Audience Insights
- Age distribution
- Geographic breakdown
- Interest categories

Access analytics: **Profile** ‚Üí **‚ò∞ Menu** ‚Üí **Analytics**

## Verification Badge

Get verified to build trust:

### Eligibility Requirements
- Active account for 30+ days
- Complete profile with photo
- Verified email/phone
- No community violations
- Authentic identity

### How to Apply
1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Account**
2. Tap **Request Verification**
3. Submit required documents:
   - Government ID
   - Proof of identity
   - Social media links (optional)
4. Wait for review (3-7 days)

:::warning[Verification is Free]
VYRAL never charges for verification. Beware of scams!
:::

## Profile Tips for Growth

### Optimize for Discovery

1. **Complete All Fields** - Full profiles rank higher
2. **Use Keywords** - Include searchable terms in bio
3. **Quality Photo** - Professional image builds trust
4. **Consistent Branding** - Match your other platforms
5. **Regular Updates** - Keep content fresh

### Engagement Strategies

- Post consistently (3-5 times/week)
- Respond to comments quickly
- Use relevant hashtags
- Share stories daily
- Go live weekly

### Creator Mode

Enable Creator Mode for extra features:

1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Account**
2. Toggle **Creator Mode**
3. Get access to:
   - Advanced analytics
   - Monetization tools
   - Creator dashboard
   - Priority support

## Profile Sections

### Featured Posts

Pin important posts to your profile:

- Maximum 3 pinned posts
- Appears at top of profile
- Great for announcements or best content

### Highlights (Stories)

Save story highlights to your profile:

1. View your expired story
2. Tap **Highlight**
3. Create or add to existing highlight
4. Add cover image and title

### Collections

Organize your posts into collections:

- Create themed galleries
- Showcase your work
- Help followers discover content
