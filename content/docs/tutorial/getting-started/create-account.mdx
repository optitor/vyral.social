## Privacy & Security Setup

### Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Protect your account with 2FA:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Tap **Enable 2FA**
3. Choose SMS or Authenticator App
4. Follow the setup wizard

:::warning[Keep Your 2FA Backup Codes]
Save your backup codes in a safe place. You'll need them if you lose access to your 2FA device.
:::

### Privacy Settings

Choose who can see your content:

- **Public** - Anyone can see your posts
- **Followers Only** - Only approved followers
- **Private** - Completely private account

## VCoin Wallet Setup

Your VYRAL account comes with a built-in VCoin wallet:

- **Custodial Wallet** - Automatically created and managed
- **Starting Balance** - New users receive welcome bonus VYC
- **Secure** - Protected by your account credentials

Learn more in [Understanding VCoin Wallet](/docs/tutorial/getting-started/vcoin-wallet).

## Troubleshooting

### Email Verification Not Received

- Check your spam/junk folder
- Make sure you entered the correct email
- Request a new verification email in Settings

### Can't Create Username

- Usernames must be unique
- 3-20 characters, letters, numbers, and underscores only
- Try variations if your desired username is taken

### Login Issues

- Reset your password via "Forgot Password"
- Clear app cache and try again
- Contact support if issues persist
