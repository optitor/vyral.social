## Sending VCoin

Send VYC to other VYRAL users:

1. Go to **Wallet** ‚Üí **Send VYC**
2. Enter recipient's username or wallet address
3. Enter amount
4. Add optional message
5. Confirm and send

:::tip[Instant Transfers]
Transfers between VYRAL users are instant and have minimal fees.
:::

## Receiving VCoin

To receive VYC from someone:

1. Share your **username** or **wallet address**
2. Find in: **Wallet** ‚Üí **Receive**
3. Show QR code or copy address
4. Funds appear instantly

## Wallet Security

### Backup Your Seed Phrase

For maximum security, backup your wallet:

1. Go to **Wallet** ‚Üí **Settings** ‚Üí **Backup Seed Phrase**
2. Write down your 12-word phrase
3. Store it safely offline
4. Never share with anyone

:::warning[Critical]
Your seed phrase is the ONLY way to recover your wallet if you lose access. Store it securely!
:::

### Security Best Practices

- ‚úÖ Enable 2FA on your account
- ‚úÖ Use strong, unique password
- ‚úÖ Backup seed phrase offline
- ‚úÖ Never share seed phrase
- ‚úÖ Verify recipient before sending large amounts
- ‚ùå Don't screenshot seed phrase
- ‚ùå Don't store seed phrase digitally

### How VYC Maintains Value

3. **Revenue Share** - 80% to creators (high demand)

## Restoring Your Wallet

If you lose access to your account:

### With Seed Phrase
1. Install VYRAL app
2. Tap **Restore Wallet**
3. Enter your 12-word seed phrase
4. Set new account password
5. Access restored

### Without Seed Phrase
If you don't have your seed phrase and lose account access, your VYC cannot be recovered. Always backup your seed phrase!
