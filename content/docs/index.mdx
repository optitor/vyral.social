### Social & Content
- **Feeds**: Algorithmic + chronological content discovery
- **Posts**: Text, photos, videos, and mixed media
- **Threads**: X/Twitter-style conversations
- **Trending**: Real-time content discovery

### Communication
- **E2E Encryption**: Military-grade secure messaging
- **Group Chats**: Private and public conversations
- **Voice Messages**: Encrypted audio sharing
- **File Sharing**: Secure document transfer

- **Marketplace**: P2P buying and selling
